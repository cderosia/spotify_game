Objective

The goal is to indentify both the title of the song and the artist(s) of the song.
The quicker you correctly guess the more points you will earn.

Setup

Select a playlist of yours that you want to play the game with.
Select the number of songs you would like to be quizzed on.

Gameplay

Right after you hit "start game", the first song starts playing.
You only get one guess per song, but you can pause the song up to 3 times.
Each time you pause the song, you have 20 seconds until the song resumes again.
After the 3rd pause, you have 20 seconds to enter your final guess.
At that time, the next song starts playing.

Earning Points

Each song has a maximum of 100 points to be earned.
If you correctly guess just the title or just the artist and not the other, then you get half points.
Points are awarded as follows:
Elapsed time <= 2 seconds: 100 points
Elapsed time <= 4 seconds: 90 points
Elapsed time <= 6 seconds: 70 points
Elapsed time <= 10 seconds 40 points
Elapsed time <= 20 seconds: 20 points
Elapsed time <= 30 seconds: 10 points
Elapsed time > 30 seconds: 0 points

Notes

You need to identify the lead artist credited with the song to get the full points.
You don't need to specify if the song is a cover, remix, acoustic, or who is featuring, etc. Just the title.
Guesses are not case sensitive (i.e., "clouds" would be acceptable for "CLOUDS")
However, spelling needs to be perfect (i.e., "Chasing You" would NOT be acceptable for "Chasin' You")
